    Our Impact

    It all began with a vision to assist people in collaborative learning and problem-solving platform. Gradually, we began conducting workshops and boot-camps, and to date, we have successfully completed 17+ workshops on diverse topics. We've assisted over 8000 people in dipping their toes into the realm of technology.

    What we do

    We conduct workshops, boot-camps, events, podcast, teach technology enthusiast and help them to dip their toes in the realm of technology around the Nation.

    We Teach Technology

    We bridge the Gap

    We bridge the gap between tech enthusiast students and industry needs by teaching them real and demanding programming skills from highly experienced industry experts.

    We help you get employed

    We assist IT students, in obtaining their dream job by providing assistance and internships in which they may demonstrate their present skills, learn, and explore new opportunities.
