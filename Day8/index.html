Organizer of CLG (Connect-Learn-Grow) ?

We’re ITSNP (IT Students of Nepal) an organization working for techies in Nepal. Established on 30th October 2020. We have been conducting free workshops over the year to fulfill the gap between IT students and the industry need by providing them the practical knowledge through our workshop on various topics in the realm of technology. It all began with a vision to assist people in collaborative learning and problem-solving platform. IT Students of Nepal (ITSNP ORG) is an organization working for the welfare of tech enthusiasts in Nepal. Gradually, we began conducting workshops and boot-camps, and to date, we have successfully completed 17+ workshops on diverse topics. We've assisted over 8000 people in dipping their toes into the realm of technology.

What is CLG?

30 days of self guided learning is an initiative by ITSNP ORG to help women tech enthusiast connect and learn new skills daily for 30 days for at least an hour a day while connecting and creating projects together with future women leaders. The main reason of CLG is to make peer-inspiration more efficient, We believe a women can guide, inspire another women for good, The learning can be any new skills like coding, personality development, designing, etc., or even enhancing the existing skills you have. The sole goal here is to learn and to form the habit of learning while making your commitment public to make it more consistent. This learning pattern will help you in three main aspects: Consistency , Peer-Inspiration, and Development.
